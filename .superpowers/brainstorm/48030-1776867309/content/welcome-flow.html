Post-Payment Welcome Flow — How Big?

What happens after someone pays and lands back on the app

A

In-App Welcome Modal

Replace the 6-second toast with a proper modal. "You're in — here's what to do first." 3 setup steps: set your watchlist, pick your brief frequency, run your first sitrep. Done in-app, no email.

Ships: April 28 · Build: ~3 hours

B

Email Onboarding Sequence

Day 1 welcome + what to do first. Day 3 "have you tried the AI analyst?" nudge. Day 7 upgrade prompt if still on free. Sent via Resend, triggered by Stripe webhook.

Ships: April 28 · Build: ~4 hours

C

Both — In-App + Email Sequence

Welcome modal on landing + a 3-email sequence in the background. The modal gets them oriented in the first 5 minutes. The emails keep them coming back over the first week. Together they drive the activation → retention loop.

Ships: April 28 · Build: ~1 day
